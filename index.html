About Me




Mitchel Clow is a man of many interests. When he's not podcasting about Buffy the Vampire Slayer, he enjoys really long road trips and thrift shopping. Born and raised in UpState NY, Mitch took a trip out to Denver and never left. When it comes to weather and entertainment, he finds the Mile High City to have the best of the best.

He has two cats, Rayne and Storm, who fill his days with happiness and love. He's always down for a good conversation and is a friendly guy.
